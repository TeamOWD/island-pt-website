05 / ABOUT

The Healing Spirit of Kōkua.

Aloha,

Kōkua is a Hawaiian word meaning "to help," but its deeper essence captures our philosophy perfectly — to give oneself to the greater good by kindly serving the community.

Born and rooted right here on Maui, our practice carries the true spirit of Kōkua, offering mobile physical therapy that addresses the root causes of your pain through our Kōkua Method.

What makes our approach different? While traditional clinics rush through 30-minute appointments with generic exercise programs, we provide personalized care as a Board Certified Clinical Specialist with advanced training in low back pain, functional movement, and custom orthotic fabrication. We've helped competitive athletes, watermen, working professionals, and countless Maui locals overcome pain when standard approaches failed.

We understand the frustration of treatments that don't deliver results. That's why we focus on precision diagnostics, clear explanations, and treatment plans tailored to your unique needs. Whether you're returning to the surf lineup, the trails of Īao Valley, or simply want to play with your grandkids without back pain, we blend clinical expertise with the spirit of Aloha, treating you as ʻOhana (family), not just another patient.

Ready to experience the Kōkua difference? Schedule your comprehensive evaluation today.
