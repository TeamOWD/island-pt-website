Teddy Zabel, DPT, OCS.

Doctor of Physical Therapy · Board-Certified OCS

From people quietly managing chronic back pain at home to Olympic athletes, professional surfers, world-champion windsurfers, paddlers, pickleball players, and CrossFitters at the highest levels of competition: what threads through every one of those cases is the same approach. 03 / OUR APPROACH

Care that listens. Treatment that lasts.

Our Approach

Pain should never decide how you live.

Whether you are recovering from surgery, managing long-standing back pain, or simply wanting to move more comfortably as you get older, we build a treatment plan tailored to your body, your story, and your goals.

Every session is one-to-one with your therapist. That means no double-booking, no shuffling between patients, no handing your rehab off to a tech. Just attentive, focused care designed to help you feel better and stay better.

We are here to help if you are dealing with
  • Persistent lower back pain or stiffness
  • Reduced mobility or balance concerns
  • Recovery after surgery or an injury
  • Treatments that never seem to last
