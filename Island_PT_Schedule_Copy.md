**\--- WHAT HAPPENS NEXT \---**

**WHAT TO EXPECT**

## **What Happens After You Submit**

### **1\. We Review Your Request**

Every request goes to our team directly. We read through what you shared, make sure we are a good match for what you are dealing with, and pull together any information we will need for your first visit.

### **2\. We Reach Out Within One Business Day**

You will hear from us by your preferred contact method within one business day — usually much sooner. We will answer any questions, walk through pricing if you want, and find a time that works for both of us.

### **3\. We Confirm Your Appointment**

Once we lock in a time, you will get a confirmation with everything you need — the address (or visit-prep instructions if it is a home visit), what to wear, and what to expect during your first session.

### **4\. Your First Visit**

First visits are 60 to 90 minutes. We will spend the time thoroughly understanding your situation, examining how you move, and giving you a clear plan to address what is driving your discomfort. You will leave knowing exactly what is going on and what to do about it.

**\--- FAQ \---**

**COMMON QUESTIONS BEFORE BOOKING**

## **Frequently Asked Questions**

**Do I need a doctor’s referral to book?**

No. Hawaiʻi is a direct-access state, so you can schedule physical therapy with us directly. (If you plan to submit a superbill for out-of-network insurance reimbursement, your plan may still require a referral — check with your insurance.)

**How soon can I get in?**

Most new patients are scheduled within a week of submitting their request. If you have something time-sensitive going on, let us know in the form and we will do our best to fit you in sooner.

**Do I have to choose a service to book?**

No. If you are not sure which service is the right fit, choose "Not sure — help me decide" on the form. We will figure it out together during our follow-up call.

**Can someone else book on my behalf?**

Yes — a family member, partner, or caregiver can submit the form for you. Just let us know in the "anything else" field who we should contact and how, and we will follow up accordingly.

**What if I need to cancel after I book?**

See our cancellation policy above. We ask for at least \[24 / 48\] hours’ notice when possible. Reschedules are easy — just reach out.

**Can I book a virtual consultation if I am not in Maui?**

Yes. Virtual consultations are available for patients beyond Maui. They are especially useful for second opinions, follow-ups, and ongoing wellness check-ins.
