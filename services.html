01 / APPROACH

A Different Kind of Physical Therapy.

Care That Listens. Treatment That Lasts.

Your time is valuable, and we understand that. Our treatments are designed to help you feel better, faster. - Not carry on indefinitely.

At Island PT, every service we offer is built on the same foundation: one-to-one care, a thorough evaluation, and a plan designed to efficiently meet your needs.

Whether you are working through chronic back pain, recovering from surgery, regaining your balance, or trying to stay strong as you get older, we treat the root cause of the issue rather than chasing the symptoms. That is the difference between feeling better for a few days and feeling better for good.

02 / SPECIALTIES

Three Focused Areas of Expert Care.

Each of our specialties shares the same philosophy: attentive, hands-on treatment that delivers lasting results.

01 · Spine & Back Care

Move with confidence, again.

Evidence-based treatment for chronic back pain, disc issues, and postural concerns. We help you move with confidence again — without surgery, without endless visits, without simply learning to live with the pain.

Most people who come to us for back pain have already tried something else. Maybe injections that wore off. Maybe a few sessions at a high-volume clinic that did not change much. Our approach is different: a full evaluation to find what is actually driving the pain, followed by a focused treatment plan to fix it at the source.

Common focus areas: Chronic pain · Disc issues · Posture · Mobility

03 · Strength-Based Therapy

Movement is medicine.

We believe movement is medicine. Through gentle, progressive strengthening, we keep your body capable and independent — at any age, at any starting point.

This is what comes after the pain is gone. Pain relief is the start, not the finish line. Strength-based therapy is how we make sure the relief lasts and your body holds up to whatever Maui life asks of it — paddles, hikes, gardening, long walks on the beach, or simply moving well into your 70s, 80s, and beyond.

Common focus areas: Active care · Gradual progress · Long-term wellness

03 / RUNEASI ASSESSMENT

Running Gait Assessment.

Specialized Service

Whether you are training for your first Hyrox event or your fifteenth marathon, our running gait assessment gives you a clear picture of how your body actually moves — and what that means for your injury risk and performance.

Using a small wearable sensor, we capture real-time data on impact loading, left-right symmetry, cadence, ground contact time, and more. In just a few minutes, you walk away with actionable insights you can use right away to run smarter, recover faster, and stay on the road longer.

What you get from the assessment
  • Impact loading rate and shock absorption analysis
  • Left and right symmetry breakdown
  • Cadence and ground contact time data
  • A personalized report with specific exercise recommendations
